Certificate

The SKZ - Cert GmbH certifies to the company

KWT GmbH
Auweg 3
35647 Waldsolms-Brandoberndorf

the introduction and application of a management system according to
ISO 50001:2018

scope:
Manufacturing and selling of pipes, fittings and accessories for water supply and
the manufacturing, trading and selling of pipes and fittings for drainage

The proof has been furnished by an audit. The EnMS refers to all energy quantities
consumed by the named company.

This certificate is valid from: 01.10.2025 to: 22.11.2027
Project No.: 000932.EQU/24.R
Certificate No.: 000932.E Würzburg, 01.10.2025

Dipl. Ing, Robert Schmitt
