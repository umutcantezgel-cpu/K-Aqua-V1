# 09 - Landing Page Strategies

This document converts the 36 distinct conversion funnels and logical architectures located in `landing.csv` into absolute strategic paradigms.

## The 36 Archetypes of Landing Pages

### 1-10: The High-Conversion Standards
1. **Hero + Features + CTA**: Standard SaaS flow. Parallax hero, feature card hover lift. CTA must have 7:1 contrast. Sticky navbar CTA.
2. **Hero + Testimonials + CTA**: Social proof focused. Testimonials must precede the primary CTA. Avatar fade-in animations.
3. **Product Demo + Features**: Interaction-first. Embedded interactive mockup or auto-play muted video center stage.
4. **Minimal Single Column**: No distractions. Direct text, single CTA. No nav clutter. Use when brand trust is already established.
5. **Funnel (3-Step Conversion)**: Step colors (Red=Problem, Orange=Process, Green=Solution). Progressive disclosure layout.
6. **Comparison Table + CTA**: "Us vs Them". Highlight product row in light yellow or green. Checkmark animations.
7. **Lead Magnet + Form**: Single intent landing. Form fields <= 3. High contrast submit button.
8. **Pricing Page + CTA**: Highlight the 'Recommended' tier with border/bg. Embed a monthly/yearly toggle animation. Show annual discount.
9. **Video-First Hero**: Dark overlay (60%) on auto-play video. White text. Boosts engagement drastically.
10. **Scroll-Triggered Storytelling**: Narrative progression via scroll. Distinct chapter color transitions. Progressive disclosure.

### 11-20: Contextual & Specialized
11. **AI Personalization**: Dynamic hero swapping based on segment. Context-aware CTA placement.
12. **Waitlist/Coming Soon**: Hero with countdown, email form above fold, social proof (number of waitlisted users). Scarcity urgency.
13. **Comparison Table Focus**: Aggressive competitive positioning. Winner CTA at bottom.
14. **Pricing-Focused Landing**: Leads with value, immediately shows pricing cards. FAQ accordion right below for objections.
15. **App Store Style Landing**: Device mockups, carousel screenshots, gold star ratings, App/Play store prominent badges.
16. **FAQ/Documentation Landing**: Huge search bar centered. Popular categories listed. Success green for 'resolved' answers.
17. **Immersive/Interactive Experience**: WebGL or 3D, gamified progression. *Must* include a skip option.
18. **Event/Conference Landing**: Date/Countdown priority, Speaker cards, Sponsor logos. Register CTA pinned.
19. **Product Review/Ratings Focused**: Trust-heavy. Stars everywhere. Individual review filtering. Verified purchase badges.
20. **Community/Forum Landing**: Warm palette. Emphasize active member metrics (Avatars, "X online").

### 21-36: Novel & Elite Archetypes
21. **Before-After Transformation**: Slider interactions. Grey vs Vibrant colors. Specific percentage metrics.
22. **Marketplace / Directory**: Massive centered search bar is the primary CTA. Minimal friction to browse.
23. **Newsletter / Content First**: Minimalist. Paper-like bg. Single field "Email" form. Author bio attached.
24. **Webinar Registration**: Live countdown timer, "Limited Seats" logic. Auto-fill timezone indicators.
25. **Enterprise Gateway**: "Path Selection" (e.g., I am a... Developer / Executive). Navy/Grey corporate palettes.
26. **Portfolio Grid**: Masonry grid. Hover overlay info. Image lazy loading is critical. Let work shine, neutral UI.
27. **Horizontal Scroll Journey**: Storytelling mapped horizontally. Floating sticky CTA tracking alongside scroll.
28. **Bento Grid Showcase**: Modular Apple-style features. Tilt effect, staggered reveals on load. Scannable value.
29. **Interactive 3D Configurator**: Product center stage. UI overlay minimal. Real-time material swaps.
30. **AI-Driven Dynamic Landing**: Instant 'Try It' input mechanism in hero section. Generating animations on click.
31. **Feature-Rich Showcase**: 4-6 deep feature cards. Strong CTA repetition after every two features.
32. **Hero-Centric Design**: Full bleed visual. 60-80% of screen above fold. Minimal text, high-impact background.
33. **Trust & Authority + Conversion**: Pure credibility. Security badges, compliance logos, transparent pricing forms.
34. **Real-Time / Operations Landing**: Live data ticker. Ops/security products. Status pulsing dots (green/amber/red).

## Structural Ordering Logic
For every landing page archetype, follow the exact structural section sequence outlined (e.g., 1. Hero, 2. Feature, 3. Social Proof). Deviation from this sequence breaks the psychological conversion mapping tested and defined in the `.csv` matrix. All structural elements must utilize the aesthetic definitions from Document 08.
