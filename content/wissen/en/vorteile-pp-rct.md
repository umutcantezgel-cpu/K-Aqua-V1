Modern building services engineering places increasingly high demands on the materials used. Particularly in drinking water and heating installations, conventional PP-R is often no longer sufficient to meet the heightened requirements for pressure, temperature, and longevity. This is where **PP-RCT (Polypropylene Random Copolymer with modified Crystallinity and Temperature resistance)** comes into play.

## What exactly is PP-RCT?

PP-RCT is an advanced development of the classic PP-R material. A special nucleation process modifies the crystal structure of the polypropylene. The result is a material that is not only more resistant to higher pressures at elevated temperatures but also offers significant hydraulic advantages.

### The 3 Decisive Advantages:

1. **Higher pressure resistance with reduced wall thickness**
   Due to the improved mechanical properties of PP-RCT, pipes can be extruded with thinner walls for the same nominal pressure (PN). This leads to a larger internal diameter and thus a higher flow capacity (up to 37% more flow).
   
2. **Excellent longevity (50+ years)**
   K-Aqua piping systems made of PP-RCT are designed for a service life of over 50 years. They effortlessly withstand temperature fluctuations and high continuous loads in both industrial and private systems.
   
3. **Freedom from corrosion and incrustation**
   Unlike metallic systems, PP-RCT pipes are absolutely immune to corrosion (rust) and incrustation (calcium deposits). The extremely smooth inner surface ensures that the pipe cross-section remains unchanged for decades, which keeps system pump performance permanently low.

## Application Areas for K-Aqua PP-RCT Systems

Because of its superior properties, PP-RCT is ideally suited for:

* **Drinking water installations:** Absolutely hygienic, odorless, and tasteless. Complies with the strictest international standards (SKZ, DVGW, KIWA).
* **Heating and cooling systems:** Excellent temperature resistance (both hot water and chilled water in HVAC systems).
* **Industrial applications:** Resistant to a variety of chemicals and aggressive media.
