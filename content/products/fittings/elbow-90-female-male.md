# Elbow 90° Female/Male

PP-R socket/spigot fusion elbow for 90° directional changes. Known frequently as a "street elbow", this unique 90-degree fitting combines a standard welding socket on one end with a male spigot on the other. This allows the fitting to be welded directly into the socket of an adjoining fitting, eliminating the need for short pipe nipples. This greatly reduces installation time, minimizes the number of weld joints, and tightens the overall footprint of piping clusters. Perfect for dense plumbing configurations in mechanical rooms or behind-the-wall installations.

## High-Density Routing Solutions
The 90° female/male elbow is an indispensable asset for high-density piping environments. Often referred to as a "street elbow", this fitting enables immediate right-angle turns directly out of another fitting, such as a tee or a valve, without inserting an intermediate pipe segment. This capability is paramount in mechanical rooms where spatial constraints dictate the entire system architecture, allowing for extremely compact parallel pipe runs.

## Structural and Weld Integrity
By halving the required number of welds for a 90-degree transition, the K-Aqua 90° street elbow inherently reduces the risk of human error during installation. The monolithic bond formed during the polyfusion process is as strong as the pipe itself. Even under maximum operational pressures, the structural integrity of this 90-degree pivot point remains absolute, ensuring zero-leak performance and maintaining the pristine hygiene of the transported fluids.
